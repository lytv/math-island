# Math Island video storyboard V2: Make 10 First

Project: Math Island kids explainer V2
Audience: ages 5-6
Core problem: 8 + 5
Core strategy: make 10 first
Target runtime: 38 seconds

## V2 goal
Keep the same teaching arc as V1, but make the world feel more like Math Island itself:
- more ocean/island/map personality
- warmer Animal Crossing-style cards and colors
- more playful counters and decorative landmarks
- more delight without adding cognitive clutter

## V2 visual upgrades
- Ocean + island blob background inspired by the actual IslandMap screen
- Sandy paths, palm trees, flowers, and little landmark/building accents
- Parchment/wooden sign cards instead of plain white cards
- Rounder, friendlier typography
- Shell counters with highlights and island color palette
- Sparkles and star stickers on reveals

## Storyboard

### Scene 1 — Arrive on Math Island
Time: 0.0s to 5.0s
Visuals:
- Ocean and island horizon
- Top sign: Math Island • Make 10 First
- Main card with 8 + 5 = ?
- Blue shell group and coral shell group sitting on a sandy clearing
Beat:
- This is a new puzzle on the island.

### Scene 2 — Eight is almost ten
Time: 5.0s to 10.0s
Visuals:
- The 8 shells sit in a ten-frame-like island path shape
- Two empty golden spots glow
- Soft arrow or sparkle hints toward the missing spaces
Beat:
- Eight only needs two more.

### Scene 3 — Split the five
Time: 10.0s to 15.0s
Visuals:
- The 5-shell group separates into a teal pair and coral trio
- A small wooden sign shows 5 = 2 + 3
Beat:
- Five can split into two and three.

### Scene 4 — Walk the two shells across the island
Time: 15.0s to 21.0s
Visuals:
- The two teal shells travel across a dotted sandy path
- The left group becomes a golden 10 row with a sparkle burst
- Caption ribbon: Make 10 first!
Beat:
- The move is the heart of the strategy.

### Scene 5 — Friendly ten solves it
Time: 21.0s to 26.0s
Visuals:
- Big cozy answer card with 10 + 3 = 13
- Star sticker / celebration accent
- Final 13 in bright island pink-red
Beat:
- Once ten is built, the answer is easy.

### Scene 6 — Two more island puzzles
Time: 26.0s to 34.0s
Visuals:
- Two mini activity cards like island map stops
- 9 + 6 -> 10 + 5 = 15
- 7 + 8 -> 10 + 5 = 15
- Small landmark icons on each mini card
Beat:
- This trick works again and again.

### Scene 7 — Your turn on the island
Time: 34.0s to 37.8s
Visuals:
- Challenge signboard: Your turn: 8 + 6 = ?
- Empty glowing spots still visible in the 10 frame
- Question bubble with a gentle pulse
Beat:
- Invite the child to solve it independently.

## V2 quality bar
- More charming than V1
- Still instantly readable
- Decorations must frame the math, not compete with it
- Every frame should feel like it belongs inside Math Island
